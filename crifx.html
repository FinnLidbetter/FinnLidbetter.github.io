CRIFX

CRIFX is the Contest Readiness Insights tool For anyone (X). Its purpose is to automatically produce pdf reports that give insights into tasks that are completed and tasks that are still outstanding in preparing an icpc-style competitive programming contest. It is installable from PyPI via pip.

For more information, visit https://github.com/FinnLidbetter/crifx.
