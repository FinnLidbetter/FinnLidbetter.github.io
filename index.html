About me

I am a mathematics and computer science enthusiast currently working on the Algorithms and Optimization team at RideCo, a Sofware as a Service company providing dynamic transit ridesharing solutions. In 2017 graduated from Mount Allison University in Sackville New Brunswick with a first class joint honours in Mathematics and Computer Science with distinction and a minor in Philosophy. I went on to complete a Master's of Mathematics in Computer Science at the University of Waterloo, and completed my degree in December 2018.
