About me

Hello, my name is Finn! I am a mathematics and computer science enthusiast currently working as a Senior Software Engineer on the Algorithms and Optimization team at RideCo. I completed a bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Computer Science with a minor in Philosophy at Mount Allison University in 2017, and a Master's of Mathematics in Computer Science at the University of Waterloo in 2018.

Competitive Programming

I have been volunteering my time as a judge for several ICPC and ICPC-style contests since 2018. I write sample solutions to problems, contribute test data for problems, and provide feedback on wording. Since 2021, I have been the Chief Judge for the Northeast North America ICPC region. A list of problems I have authored can be found here: https://open.kattis.com/problem-authors/Finn%20Lidbetter.

Other contests that I have contributed to as a problem author or judge include:

  • The Mount Allison Programming Showdown (MAPS)
  • The Atlantic Canadian Preliminary Contest
  • The North American Championship
